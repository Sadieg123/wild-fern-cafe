About Wild Fern Café

More than just coffee—we're your neighborhood gathering place

Our Story

Wild Fern Café started with a simple idea: what if there was a coffee shop that felt less like a business and more like home? In 2019, founders Maya Chen and David Torres transformed a former bookshop on Maple Street into the cozy café you see today. They filled the space with plants, reclaimed furniture, and the warm aroma of fresh coffee, creating a welcoming environment where neighbors could connect over a great cup of joe. The name "Wild Fern" comes from Maya's grandmother's kitchen plant—a Boston fern that grew with wild abandon, symbolizing the natural growth and community they hoped to cultivate.

What We Stand For

Our mission is simple: serve exceptional coffee with genuine hospitality while building a stronger, more connected community. Here's what guides us every day:

Quality First

We never cut corners. From sourcing single-origin beans to training our baristas in proper technique, we believe great coffee requires attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

Support Local

Our beans come from Portland roasters, our milk from nearby dairy farms, and our pastries are baked in-house daily. When you support us, you're supporting a whole network of local producers.

Sustainability Matters

We're working toward zero waste with compostable cups, reusable dishware, and a discount for bringing your own mug. Small steps add up to big change.

Everyone's Welcome

Whether you're here for a quick coffee or settling in for hours, you're part of our community. We believe cafés should be gathering places for all people.

The Wild Fern Experience

Step inside and you'll find more than just a café—you'll discover a space designed for connection and comfort. Sunlight streams through tall windows, illuminating rows of thriving plants and well-worn wooden tables that invite you to stay awhile. Our community table is always open for students, remote workers, or anyone who needs a productive space outside the house. We host monthly open mic nights, rotate artwork from local artists on our walls, and genuinely enjoy getting to know our regulars (though we love meeting new faces too!).

We believe coffee shops should be anchors in their neighborhoods—places where people bump into friends, where ideas are born over lattes, and where you can always count on a friendly face. That's the Wild Fern difference, and it's what keeps bringing people back day after day.
