Our Menu

Handcrafted with care, served with love

At Wild Fern Café, we believe every item on our menu should be something we're proud to serve. Our coffee is sourced from local roasters and brewed to perfection, our pastries are baked fresh each morning, and our food is made with quality ingredients. Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a leisurely breakfast, we've got something delicious waiting for you.
